Press Hit to draw a card. Keep drawing cards until you get a number closer to but not more than 21. If you get a number less than 21, you can press Stand to let the dealer (your opponent) play. If your cards sum up to a number more than 21, you bust (you lose) and the dealer plays. If the sum of your cards is exactly 21 (which is even higher than 21), then you get Blackjack. If you get blackjack and the dealer also gets blackjack, then it's a push (you draw). When playing blackjack, the one with the higher number wins.
You can press H to hit and also S (on the keyboard) to stand
